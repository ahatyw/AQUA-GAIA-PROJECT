## Project Overview

**AQUA-GAIA** is a **Global Aquatic Digital Twin** that monitors and predicts water health worldwide. The platform provides a **3D interactive Earth model** visualizing water quality, pollution levels, and freshwater availability across countries. It allows stakeholders, researchers, and policymakers to gain actionable insights into global aquatic systems.

---

## Motivation

Water scarcity and pollution are critical challenges affecting millions globally. Existing water monitoring systems are often static, delayed, and fragmented. **AQUA-GAIA** aims to create a dynamic, interactive digital twin of the Earth's aquatic systems, enabling **real-time insights**, **predictive analytics**, and **educational visualization** of water health globally.
